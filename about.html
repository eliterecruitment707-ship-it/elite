About Us

Elite Recruitment Agency is a trusted international recruitment firm dedicated to connecting skilled and unskilled workers with verified employers across the Middle East including Qatar, Kuwait, and the United Arab Emirates.

We specialize in overseas job placements in sectors such as construction, hospitality, security, customer service, and domestic services. Our mission is to provide reliable, transparent, and efficient recruitment solutions to both job seekers and employers.
